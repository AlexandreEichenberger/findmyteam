Mission

Our mission is to foster local FIRST communities by providing a platform that connects interested individuals, teams, mentors, and outreach organizations. Using this website, individuals may find a suitable local team, teams may recruit new members and mentors, and charitable organiation may find teams to help them having an impact on the local communities.

Our goal is to develop a secure method for individuals , teams, and organizations to connect without sharing any confidential information until mutual consent is given. In order to guarantee the highest level of security, we developped our platform with professional-grade web-technology that is currently used by NASA, Instagram, and Pinterest, among others.
